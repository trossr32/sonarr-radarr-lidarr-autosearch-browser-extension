Sonarr · Radarr · Lidarr · Readarr

Every show, film and album — one click from anywhere on the web.

Servarr Auto Search is a free, open-source browser extension that spots media on the sites you already browse and beams it straight into your *arr apps. No copying, no pasting, no tab juggling.

The URL trick

Type a title onto the end of your search URL. Done.

Append anything to your Servarr /add/new page and the extension fills the search box and fires the search for you — perfect for bookmarklets, launchers and muscle memory.

sonarr — add new series
$ open http://sonarr:8989/add/new/severance
→ search field populated · search triggered · showing results for "severance"

Three ways in

Search your *arrs without leaving the page.

Site search icons

On IMDb, TMDb, Trakt, Letterboxd, MusicBrainz and 15 more sites, the extension injects a Servarr icon next to the title. One click opens the right app with the right search — often by exact ID.

Right-click search

Highlight any title anywhere on the web, right-click, and send it to Sonarr, Radarr, Lidarr or Readarr from the context menu. A new tab lands on the results.

URL auto-search

Open …/add/new/fringe on your instance and the search runs itself. Provide an API key and the extension even auto-detects your Servarr version and selectors.
